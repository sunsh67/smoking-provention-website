How Our Lecture Effects
Overview
Our present day youth is exposed to E-cigarette which is relatively new way in delivering the addictive substance nicotine, without burning tobacco. This new method targeting young population raises serious concerns, because e-cigarettes could be an entryway to nicotine addiction and increases the danger of lung diseases. For this capstone project, group of student from Seattle Central College Respiratory Care Program (
Minsoo K, Tim H, Jessica C, & Cyril C
) have made a curriculum to teach young adolescent population a dangers of tobacco, marijuana, e-cigarettes, and correlate with Chronic Obstructive Pulmonary Disease (COPD). Answer for this issue is simple:
Be smart, Don't start.
